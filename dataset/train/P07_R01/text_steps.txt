Turn on your espresso coffee machine to allow it to heat up the water.
Clean and Dry Portafilter & Basket. Before making a coffee with the espresso machine make sure that your portafilter & basket are both clean and dry.
Dose your ground coffee into the portafilter.
Using a coffee tamper, tamp your coffee. Then apply a light amount of pressure to compact your coffee.
Turn on the kettle
Make The Espresso
Add the boiling water from the kettle
