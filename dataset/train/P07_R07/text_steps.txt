Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Wash and peel the sweet potatoes and chop into bite-sized pieces. Put the sweet potatoes in a bowl and add the olive oil, cumin, and chili powder. Mix well and pour the mixture onto a baking tray. Roast for 10 minutes.
Pulse all the dressing ingredients in a food processor until mostly smooth.
Check the sweet potatoes and roast for another 10-15 minutes.
Wash and then chop and tear the lettuce into a bowl.
Add the sweetcorn and beans to the bowl. Chop the rest of the avocado and add.
Remove the sweet potatoes from the oven.
Stir the salad and add the dressing. Stir again.
Add the sweet potatoes.
