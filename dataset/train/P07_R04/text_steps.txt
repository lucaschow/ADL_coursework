Measure the butter into a microwavable container, and heat it in the microwave. Stir.
In a large bowl, combine the melted butter and sugar. Mix to combine.
Place the banana into a microwavable container, and heat it in the microwave. Stir.
Add the banana to the melted butter and sugar. Mix to combine.
Separate an egg yolk and add.
Add vanilla extract. Mix well.
Measure and add the flour, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon. Stir until combined and no streaks of flour remain.
Last, fold in the chocolate chips. Set aside to let the flour hydrate in the dough.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Use a cookie scoop or a spoon to scoop out about 1 inch dough balls. Place 2 inches apart on the prepared cookie sheet
Put in the oven and bake for 10 to 11 minutes or until the edges are set and the cookies have puffed up, but the middles are still underbaked.
Remove the cookies from the oven
