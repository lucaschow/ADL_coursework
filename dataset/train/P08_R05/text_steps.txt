Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C/gas mark 4.
Place salmon teriyaki skewers on a baking sheet. Roast for 11 to 15 minutes, until just cooked. Set aside until cool enough to handle.
Pre-heat air fryer to 180 degrees C
Place broccoli in air fryer and roast for 15 minutes. Season with oil, salt, pepper, oregano, and garlic granules half-way through.
Add pre-made Chickpea, Spinach & Quinoa salad to plate.
Add broccoli and salmon skewers to plate ontop of salad. Serve and enjoy!
