Wash the kale, dry it and spread it evenly in a baking tray
Add the oil and the salt, mixing everything well. The quantities are fairly loose: just make sure the leaves are coated and sprinkle with a bit of salt.
Cook for 15 minutes in an oven preheated at 180 degrees with the fan on (200 if the oven does not have a fan). Once it's mainly wilted but crisp at the edges, it's ready.
