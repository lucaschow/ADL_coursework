Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C
Place fishcakes on baking tray and place in over for 30 minutes
Place frozen broccoli and green beans to air fryer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper half-way through.
Add couscous and boiling water to pot (no flame needed), leave until water has been absorbed
Slice onion, chili pepper, and bell pepper and place to side
Add oil to a nonstick pan. Bring to medium heat.
Once warm, add garlic, onions, red peppers and chilis to pan. Cook until browned
Add broccoli and green beans to pan and stir
Season pan with salt, pepper, and sesame oil
Serve fishcakes on top of couscous with side of vegetables
