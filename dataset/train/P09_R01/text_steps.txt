Heat some oil in a pan (any neutral oil will do) along with some salt and once heated, and red curry paste to the oil and allow to cook, ensuring it doesn't burn or darken
Add the chicken chunks in (they should have been cooked 80%)
Mix all and stir and don't let it burn
Add coconut milk to the mix.
Stir and mix and wait until it begins to boil
Add in the chopped vegetables (courgette, bamboo shoots and chili. Chili is optional)
Make sure all vegetables are beneath the liquid's surface. Stir and allow to boil.
Add in some Thai basil and turn off the cooker.
