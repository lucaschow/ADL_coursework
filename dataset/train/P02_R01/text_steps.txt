Preheat the pan over medium low heat then add 2 tablespoons olive oil.
Add the de-seeded, chopped tomatoes and cook slowly until softened.
Add the onions and chopped garlic and brown slowly until tender and golden, about 5 minutes.
Add the tomato puree and cook out for 5 minutes
Add more oil if necessary and add mushrooms. Do not raise heat. Cook an additional 5minutes.
Add the sweetcorn and season with Worcestershire sauce and apple cider vinegar. Cook for 5 more minutes.
