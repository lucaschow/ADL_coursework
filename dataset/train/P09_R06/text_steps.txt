Slice the onion, tomato and peppers into preferred size
Marinate beef in ginger and garlic paste
Thread the beef and sliced vegetables (onion, tomato and scotch bonnet chilli) onto skewers and arrange them on a baking tray
Drizzle skewers with oil
Cook in the oven for 15 minutes or more based on your oven settings, turn them over halfway through.
Peel the potatoes and cut them onto strips
Heat the oil in a pan, season it with salt, and add the potatoes in batches
Once fried, transfer to a plate and season with peri-peri seasoning
