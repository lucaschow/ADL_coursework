Start by mixing the melted butter, sweetened condensed milk with the cocoa powder. Combine them really well together, but it's okay if there are a few small lumps.
Crumble up the biscuit and combine with the mixture
Next wrap the log with foil to help it maintain its shape and place it in the fridge for at least 5 hours (depending on your freezer)
After roughly 2 hours, divide into chunks of your desired thickness.
