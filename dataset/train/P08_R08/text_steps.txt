Heat the oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes, stirring often, until the onion softens.
Add the garlic and chilli peppers and cook for a further minute.
Add the minced beef and cook for 5-6 minutes, until browned. Stir often, breaking up any large chunks of meat with a spatula.
Add the carrot, celery, bell peppers, and mushrooms and cook for a further 2-3 minutes.
Add the tomato pasta sauce, oregano, tinned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Stir and bring to the boil. Turn down the heat and simmer for 15 -20 minutes until thickened.
