Before making a coffee with the espresso machine make sure that your portafilter & basket are both clean and dry.
Dose your ground coffee into the portafilter.
Using a coffee tamper, tamp your coffee. Then apply a light amount of pressure to compact your coffee.
Put the portafilter back into the machine.
Turn on the coffee machine
Turn on the kettle
Make The Espresso
Add boiling water to the espresso
