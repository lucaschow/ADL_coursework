Wash and boil the cut potato halves in a pressure cooker. Once it is cooled, peel it and smash it. Also, keep all other ingredients ready.
Take a frying pan on medium heat, add 4tbsp of olive oil and mustard seeds. Once the mustard starts spluttering, add urad dal into the pan. Saute it for a while. When the urad dal turns brown add slit green chili`, and grated ginger. Saute it for few seconds.
Then add chopped onion, along with 1 tbsp oil and salt and then continue frying until the onions turn soft. Add turmeric powder, saute for a few seconds. Remove the pan from the heat and allow contents to cool.
Now add smashed potato to the pan and mix until finely blended. Once it is mixed, add salt to taste. Garnish with coriander leaves.
