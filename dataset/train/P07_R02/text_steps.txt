Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
Rinse the chickpeas, then place the chickpeas on a towel and lightly towel dry them so they aren't wet from rinsing. You can remove any loose skins at this time too.
Place the chickpeas on a baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil. Toss to coat, then sprinkle with the paprika, garlic powder, oregano, salt and pepper. Stir the chickpeas and shake the pan a few times to distribute the seasoning. Put the tray in the oven and roast the chickpeas for 15 minutes.
Take apart the head of lettuce. Wash the lettuce leaves and squeeze to dry.
Stir the chickpeas and roast for 10 to 15 more minutes
Tear the lettuce and place it in a large bowl.
Wash the tomatoes and then cut them in half.
Add the tomatoes to the bowl
Chop the cucumber into small pieces and add it to the bowl.
Drain the olives, tear them in half and add them to the bowl.
Crumble the blue cheese into the bowl.
Stir the ingredients in the bowl together to mix.
Remove the chickpeas from the oven and discard any burnt chickpeas.
Mix the salad and the chickpeas together in a bowl to serve.
